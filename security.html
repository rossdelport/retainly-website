Security

Last updated: 29 May 2026

Patient data is sensitive. We take security seriously at every layer of the Retainly platform — from the infrastructure we run on to the way your team authenticates.

🔒
Encryption in transit
All data between your browser, our servers, and patient apps is encrypted with TLS 1.3.
🗄️
Encryption at rest
Databases and backups are encrypted with AES-256. Encryption keys are rotated regularly.
🏗️
Cloud infrastructure
Hosted on SOC 2 Type II certified cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime SLA.
🔑
Access controls
Role-based permissions, SSO support, and enforced multi-factor authentication for clinic accounts.
📋
Audit logs
Every action taken within your clinic dashboard is logged with a timestamp and user record.
🛡️
Penetration testing
We conduct third-party penetration tests annually and remediate all critical findings promptly.

GDPR & Data Protection

Retainly is fully GDPR compliant. We act as a data processor on behalf of your clinic (the data controller). Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) are available to all clinic partners on request.

Vulnerability Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@retainly.io. We aim to acknowledge all reports within 24 hours and resolve critical issues within 72 hours.

Questions

For any security-related questions, contact us at security@retainly.io or via our contact page.
